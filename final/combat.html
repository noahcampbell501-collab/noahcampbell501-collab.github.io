Combat is arguably the most important part of Dungeons and Dragons. It has the most rules dedicated to it, it was the only way people were able to level up until 2014, and everyone expects it to happen when you play this game, regardless of party dynamics.


Unlike most of this guide, combat is something that very seamlessly translates from being a player to being a Dungeon Master. I would even argue that it's a necessity. Otherwise, you get people trying to homebrew the living daylights out of combat, because they don't know how to play normally.
